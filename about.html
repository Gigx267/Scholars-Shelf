Scholars' Shelf: where intellectual journeys begin, and the boundless horizons of understanding unfold

Unlock academic insight at Veritas University with Scholars' Shelf. This case study explores the potential integration of "Scholars' Shelf," a tailored digital library platform, within Veritas University, Abuja. Our investigation will analyze the university's current library infrastructure, resource access methods, and the specific needs of its users. The aim is to propose a strategic implementation plan for "Scholars' Shelf" and evaluate its anticipated success based on key metrics: enhanced resource accessibility, improved remote access capabilities, streamlined material discoverability, increased overall platform usage, heightened user satisfaction, and a positive impact on the university's research output.
Empowering Veritas University's Intellectual Journey:
Imagine a digital space where Veritas University's students and faculty can effortlessly explore a curated collection of research books, designed to ignite intellectual curiosity and fuel groundbreaking research. "Scholars' Shelf" offers this world of academic insight. Discover fresh perspectives, gain access to essential resources, and elevate your research endeavors through our intuitive platform. Save your progress seamlessly and pinpoint crucial information with ease. More than just a repository of knowledge, "Scholars' Shelf" empowers Veritas University's community to become pioneers in their respective fields. Our carefully selected collection of research materials is designed to spark innovation, foster critical thinking, and drive transformative research across disciplines. Navigate a seamless digital experience, uncover hidden gems of knowledge, and unlock the full potential of your intellectual capabilities.

Open access

We believe that research should have as wide a reach as possible and be put to use in making a difference in supporting social change and social justice. As a mission-led, not for profit publisher our goal is to move as much of our research-based publishing to open access as possible, especially work focused on the global social challenges.
Our monographs, multi-authored and edited works include original scholarly research, thorough and structured reviews of important subjects and engaging works that push forward the boundaries of the disciplines in which we publish
Please note, we can only accept from institutional librarians. If you are not a librarian but would like your institution to take up please consider emailing your librarian with your request. All requests will need to be sent from an institutional email address.
